In my list, I describe my traits as friendly, serious, good at listening, integrity, and hesitating. Friendly: None of us exists in this world independently. Helping others within my ability not only brings convenience to others but also makes me feel happy. At the same time, I could make more friends because of friendliness.

Serious: When I do a thing, I will take a serious attitude to do it. It is easy to get trust, whatever in friends or works because of this trait. Others will feel relieved to leave things to me. Good at listening: My friends prefer to talk to me because I am good at listening. Sometimes they need advice; sometimes, they need a person to listen to their thoughts. Listening is the first step. Then I can think from their perspective.

Integrity: This is an essential trait for me. I also believe that honesty is the basic principle of a person. I will do what I promised. If you don't speak credibly, then next time, people will be less to trust you. Hesitating: I do not act decisively enough. It should be a bad feature for me. It will directly cause me procrastinating. For example, I had an idea to go to college this idea a few years ago, but I have hesitated, worried about my language, and wasted time. When deciding to something, do it. For this, I think it needs to change.
